### Solving Fragmented Infrastructure Visibility Across Hybrid Cloud Clusters in Manufacturing with Salesforce
Manufacturing companies are under increasing pressure to optimize their operations, improve efficiency, and reduce costs. This can lead to reduced productivity, increased downtime, and decreased customer satisfaction.

#### The Problem of Fragmented Infrastructure Visibility
In today's digital age, manufacturing companies rely on a complex network of on-premise and cloud-based infrastructure to manage their operations. This includes enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems, customer relationship management (CRM) systems, and supply chain management (SCM) systems, among others. However, when these systems are deployed across multiple hybrid cloud clusters, it can create a fragmented infrastructure visibility problem.

Fragmented infrastructure visibility occurs when different systems and applications are not integrated, making it difficult to get a unified view of the entire operation. This can lead to:

* Reduced visibility into production workflows and supply chain operations
* Increased risk of errors and downtime due to lack of coordination between systems
* Difficulty in scaling operations to meet changing demand
* Inability to make data-driven decisions due to lack of access to real-time data
