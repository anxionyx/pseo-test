#### The Challenges of Distributed Software Engineering Teams
Distributed software engineering teams are becoming increasingly common in the retail industry, as companies seek to leverage global talent and reduce costs. However, managing these teams can be complex, with team members often working in different locations, time zones, and cultures. Inefficient resource allocation is a significant challenge in these teams, as it can lead to:

* **Underutilization of resources**: Team members may not be fully utilized, leading to wasted productivity and increased costs.
* **Overallocation of resources**: Team members may be overworked, leading to burnout and reduced productivity.
* **Lack of visibility**: Managers may not have real-time visibility into team members' workloads, making it difficult to allocate resources effectively.
* **Security risks**: Inefficient resource allocation can lead to security risks, as team members may have access to sensitive data and systems that they do not need.
