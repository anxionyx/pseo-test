* **Poor customer satisfaction**: Customers may become frustrated with the lack of progress or attention to their concerns, leading to negative reviews and churn.
* **Inefficient resource allocation**: Support teams may spend more time and resources on resolving issues that could have been prevented or addressed earlier with proper feedback mechanisms.
