#### The Problem: Inefficient Resource Allocation
In AdTech, distributed software engineering teams often work on complex systems, managing multiple applications, services, and infrastructure components. Without proper monitoring and visibility, it's easy to misallocate resources, leading to:
* Underutilized servers and instances
* Overprovisioned infrastructure
* Inefficient use of engineering time and talent
* Reduced system performance and increased latency

These inefficiencies can result in wasted resources, decreased productivity, and a negative impact on the bottom line.
