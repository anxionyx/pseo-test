### Breaking Down Data Silos in Real Estate Tech: How Okta Can Help
The real estate industry has undergone significant transformations in recent years, driven by technological advancements and changing consumer behaviors. As a result, real estate companies are leveraging a wide range of tools and platforms to manage their operations, from legacy CRM systems to modern marketing tools. However, this has created a new challenge: data silos.

#### What are Data Silos?
Data silos refer to the isolation of data within different systems, making it difficult to access, share, and analyze. In the context of real estate tech, data silos can occur when legacy CRM systems are not integrated with modern marketing tools, resulting in fragmented customer data, inefficient workflows, and missed opportunities.

#### The Impact of Data Silos on Real Estate Businesses
Data silos can have significant consequences for real estate businesses, including:

* **Inefficient customer management**: With customer data scattered across different systems, it can be challenging to get a unified view of customer interactions, preferences, and behaviors.
* **Missed marketing opportunities**: Without access to comprehensive customer data, marketing teams may struggle to create targeted campaigns, leading to lower conversion rates and reduced ROI.
* **Inadequate analytics and insights**: Data silos can limit the ability to analyze customer data, making it difficult to identify trends, patterns, and areas for improvement.
