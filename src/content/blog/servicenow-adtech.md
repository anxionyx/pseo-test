### Disconnected Customer Feedback Loops: A Major Pain Point in AdTech Customer Support Workflows
The AdTech industry is rapidly evolving, with new technologies and innovations emerging every day. However, one challenge that remains a constant thorn in the side of AdTech companies is the issue of disconnected customer feedback loops in customer support workflows. In this blog post, we'll explore the problems caused by disconnected feedback loops and how ServiceNow can help AdTech companies streamline their customer support workflows and improve customer satisfaction.

#### The Problem of Disconnected Feedback Loops
In AdTech, customer support teams often rely on multiple tools and systems to manage customer interactions, from ticketing systems to CRM software. However, these systems are often siloed, making it difficult for support teams to access the information they need to resolve customer issues efficiently. This can lead to disconnected feedback loops, where customer feedback is not properly captured, analyzed, or acted upon. As a result, AdTech companies may struggle to identify and address the root causes of customer complaints, leading to prolonged resolution times, decreased customer satisfaction, and ultimately, lost revenue.

#### The Consequences of Disconnected Feedback Loops
The consequences of disconnected feedback loops can be severe. For example:
* **Prolonged resolution times**: Without access to complete and accurate customer information, support teams may take longer to resolve issues, leading to frustrated customers and increased support costs.
* **Decreased customer satisfaction**: When customer feedback is not properly captured or acted upon, customers may feel that their concerns are not being heard, leading to decreased satisfaction and loyalty.
* **Lost revenue**: Disconnected feedback loops can lead to a lack of insight into customer needs and preferences, making it difficult for AdTech companies to develop targeted marketing campaigns and retain customers.
