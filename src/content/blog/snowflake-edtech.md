#### The Challenges of Manual Payroll Reconciliation
Manual reconciliation of payroll involves a plethora of tasks, including data collection, currency conversion, tax compliance, and payment processing. When dealing with multiple currencies and countries, the complexity increases manifold. Some of the key challenges EdTech companies face include:
* **Data Integrity**: Ensuring accuracy and consistency across different data sources and systems is a significant challenge.
* **Currency Fluctuations**: Managing currency exchange rates and their impact on payroll can be daunting.
* **Compliance**: Staying abreast of changing tax laws and regulations in different countries is crucial to avoid penalties and fines.
* **Scalability**: As the organization grows, manual processes can become increasingly cumbersome and difficult to manage.
