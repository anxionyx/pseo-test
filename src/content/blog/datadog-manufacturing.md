### Simplifying Fragmented Infrastructure Visibility in Hybrid Cloud Clusters for Manufacturers
Manufacturing companies are increasingly adopting hybrid cloud strategies to enhance agility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. However, this transition often leads to fragmented infrastructure visibility, making it challenging for IT teams to monitor and manage their complex environments. In this blog post, we'll explore how Datadog can help manufacturers overcome these challenges and achieve unified visibility across their hybrid cloud clusters.

#### The Challenges of Fragmented Infrastructure Visibility
Manufacturing companies typically rely on a combination of on-premises data centers, private clouds, and public clouds to support their operations. This hybrid approach can lead to a fragmented infrastructure, with different teams managing different parts of the environment. As a result, IT teams may struggle to:

* Monitor performance and availability across disparate systems
* Identify and troubleshoot issues in a timely manner
* Ensure compliance with industry regulations and standards
* Optimize resource utilization and reduce costs

#### The Consequences of Inadequate Visibility
Inadequate visibility into hybrid cloud infrastructure can have significant consequences for manufacturers, including:

* Downtime and lost productivity due to undetected issues
* Increased risk of security breaches and data loss
* Inefficient resource utilization, leading to wasted costs
* Difficulty meeting compliance requirements, resulting in fines and reputational damage
