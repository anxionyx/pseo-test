#### The Challenge of Microservices Orchestration
Microservices architecture has become the norm in software development, allowing for greater flexibility and scalability. However, as the number of microservices increases, so does the complexity of managing and monitoring them. Climate tech companies often have multiple microservices working together to achieve a common goal, such as monitoring carbon emissions or optimizing renewable energy output. But with each microservice having its own logging system, it can be difficult to get a unified view of what's happening across the entire system.

#### The Importance of Unified Audit Logs
Unified audit logs are essential for climate tech companies to ensure transparency, accountability, and security. With a unified view of all logs, companies can:

* **Monitor system performance**: Identify bottlenecks and areas for improvement, ensuring that systems are running efficiently and effectively.
* **Detect security threats**: Quickly identify potential security threats and take action to prevent them.
* **Meet compliance requirements**: Easily demonstrate compliance with regulatory requirements, such as GDPR or HIPAA.
