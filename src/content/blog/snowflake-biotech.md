#### The Challenges of Legacy Testing Frameworks
Traditional testing frameworks are often designed for smaller, more straightforward data sets. However, biotech data is typically large, complex, and heterogeneous, making it difficult to manage and analyze using legacy frameworks. These frameworks can create bottlenecks in several areas, including:
* **Data ingestion**: Legacy frameworks often struggle to handle the volume and variety of biotech data, leading to slow data ingestion and processing times.
* **Data storage**: Traditional frameworks may not be optimized for storing and managing large amounts of data, resulting in increased storage costs and decreased performance.
* **Data analysis**: Legacy frameworks can make it difficult to perform complex data analysis, such as genomic sequencing or machine learning model training, due to limited scalability and flexibility.
