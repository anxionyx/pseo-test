#### The Challenges of Manual Reconciliation
Manual reconciliation of multi-currency cross-border payroll involves a multitude of tasks, including:
* Collecting and processing payroll data from various countries and currencies
* Converting currencies and calculating exchange rates
* Reconciling payroll data with accounting records
* Identifying and resolving discrepancies
* Ensuring compliance with local regulations and laws

This manual process can be tedious, error-prone, and may lead to delays in payroll processing. Moreover, as the company grows and expands into new markets, the complexity of payroll operations increases, making it even more challenging to manage manually.
