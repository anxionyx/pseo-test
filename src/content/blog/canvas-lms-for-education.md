However, one of the significant challenges educators face is tracking student engagement in online learning environments. ## Understanding the Importance of Student Engagement
Student engagement is critical to the success of any learning program. When students are engaged, they are more likely to be motivated, retain information better, and achieve their academic goals. In asynchronous learning, where students work independently, it can be difficult for educators to gauge student engagement and identify areas where students may need additional support. Canvas LMS provides a range of features that enable educators to track student engagement, including analytics and reporting tools that offer insights into student activity and performance.
