### Simplifying Fragmented Infrastructure Visibility Across Hybrid Cloud Clusters with Notion for HR Tech
#### Introduction to the Challenge
In the modern landscape of HR technology, organizations are increasingly adopting hybrid cloud strategies to leverage the benefits of both public and private cloud environments. This approach allows for greater flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. However, it also introduces a significant challenge: managing and maintaining visibility across fragmented infrastructure. For HR teams, this can be particularly daunting, as it affects not only the IT infrastructure but also the sensitive data and applications that are crucial for human resource management. 

#### The Problem of Fragmented Infrastructure Visibility
Fragmented infrastructure visibility refers to the lack of a unified view and control over the disparate components of an organization's IT infrastructure, which are spread across different cloud platforms, on-premise data centers, and sometimes even edge locations. This fragmentation can lead to several issues, including:
- **Difficulty in Monitoring and Troubleshooting**: Without a clear view of the infrastructure, identifying and resolving issues becomes a complex and time-consuming process.
- **Inefficient Resource Allocation**: Lack of visibility can result in underutilization or overutilization of resources, leading to wasted investments and decreased productivity.
- **Security Risks**: Fragmented infrastructure increases the attack surface, making it harder to detect and respond to security threats in a timely manner.
- **Compliance Challenges**: Maintaining compliance with regulatory requirements becomes more complicated when the infrastructure is not transparent and manageable.
