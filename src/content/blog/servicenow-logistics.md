However, many logistics companies struggle with bottlenecks in their Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) pipelines, hindering their ability to deliver high-quality services quickly. One common culprit behind these bottlenecks is the use of legacy testing frameworks. In this blog post, we'll explore how ServiceNow can help logistics companies overcome these challenges and streamline their CI/CD pipelines.

#### The Problem with Legacy Testing Frameworks
Legacy testing frameworks can be a major roadblock in CI/CD pipelines. These outdated frameworks often rely on manual testing, which is time-consuming and prone to errors. Moreover, they may not be compatible with modern technologies and tools, making it difficult to integrate them into automated testing pipelines. As a result, logistics companies may experience:

* **Delays in deployment**: Manual testing can take days or even weeks, slowing down the deployment of new features and updates.
* **Increased costs**: The need for manual testing requires significant resources, including personnel, equipment, and infrastructure.
* **Reduced quality**: Manual testing is error-prone, which can lead to poor quality releases and a negative impact on customer satisfaction.
