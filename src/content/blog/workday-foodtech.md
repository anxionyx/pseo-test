#### The Challenges of GDPR Compliance in Multi-Tenant Environments
GDPR compliance requires companies to implement robust data protection measures, ensure transparency, and provide individuals with control over their personal data. In multi-tenant environments, where multiple companies share the same cloud infrastructure, ensuring GDPR compliance can be particularly complex. FoodTech companies must navigate the intricacies of data localization, access controls, and data encryption, all while maintaining the scalability and flexibility that cloud infrastructure provides.
