Inefficient resource allocation can have a significant impact on AdTech companies, leading to:

So, what are the root causes of inefficient resource allocation in AdTech? Some common issues include:
* **Lack of Visibility**: Without clear visibility into resource utilization, it's difficult for AdTech companies to make informed decisions about allocation.

* **Inconsistent Processes**: Inconsistent processes and workflows can make it difficult to allocate resources effectively, leading to confusion and inefficiency.
