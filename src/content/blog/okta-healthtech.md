### Solving Disconnected Customer Feedback Loops in HealthTech Customer Support Workflows with Okta
The HealthTech industry has witnessed significant growth in recent years, with the global market expected to reach $504.4 billion by 2025. As HealthTech companies continue to innovate and expand their services, they must prioritize customer experience to stay competitive. However, many HealthTech companies struggle with disconnected customer feedback loops in their customer support workflows, leading to poor customer satisfaction and loyalty. In this blog post, we will explore the challenges of disconnected customer feedback loops and how Okta can help HealthTech companies solve this problem.

#### The Challenges of Disconnected Customer Feedback Loops
Customer feedback is essential for HealthTech companies to improve their products and services. However, when customer feedback is not properly collected, analyzed, and acted upon, it can lead to a disconnected customer feedback loop. This occurs when customer feedback is not integrated into the customer support workflow, resulting in a lack of visibility, poor response times, and inadequate resolution of customer issues.

Some common challenges of disconnected customer feedback loops in HealthTech customer support workflows include:

* **Lack of integration**: Customer feedback is often collected through multiple channels, such as email, social media, and phone calls. However, this feedback is not integrated into the customer support workflow, making it difficult for support teams to access and act upon.
* **Poor visibility**: Customer feedback is not visible to all stakeholders, including support teams, product managers, and executives. This lack of visibility makes it challenging to identify trends and patterns in customer feedback.
* **Inadequate analysis**: Customer feedback is not properly analyzed, making it difficult to identify areas for improvement.
* **Slow response times**: Customer feedback is not responded to in a timely manner, leading to poor customer satisfaction and loyalty.
