#### The Challenges of Legacy Testing Frameworks
Legacy testing frameworks can be cumbersome, inflexible, and difficult to maintain. They often require significant manual effort, are prone to errors, and can lead to prolonged testing cycles. These frameworks can also struggle to keep up with the pace of modern software development, where speed, agility, and automation are essential. As a result, HealthTech organizations may experience:

* **Prolonged time-to-market**: Legacy testing frameworks can delay the release of new features and updates, giving competitors an edge in the market.
* **Increased costs**: Manual testing and maintenance of legacy frameworks can be costly and resource-intensive.
* **Reduced quality**: The lack of automation and efficiency in legacy frameworks can lead to a higher likelihood of errors and defects in the software.
