Traditional paperwork in real estate transactions involves a significant amount of manual labor, including printing, scanning, and faxing documents. This process can lead to delays, lost documents, and decreased productivity. Moreover, the use of paper-based documents can result in errors, such as missed signatures, incorrect dates, and incomplete information. These errors can lead to costly rework, damaging the reputation of real estate professionals and causing frustration for clients.
