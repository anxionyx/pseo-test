#### Understanding High Latency in Real-Time Inventory Synchronization
High latency refers to the delay between the time data is updated in the source system and when it is reflected in the target system. In the context of real estate tech, this can mean that property listings are not updated in real-time, resulting in potential buyers seeing outdated information. This can lead to a range of problems, including:
* Missed sales opportunities due to incorrect pricing or availability information
* Poor user experience due to outdated property listings
* Difficulty in maintaining data consistency across multiple platforms
