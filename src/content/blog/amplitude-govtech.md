### The Challenge of Legacy Testing Frameworks

Legacy testing frameworks are a common hindrance to efficient CI/CD pipelines. These frameworks, designed and implemented years ago, often lack the flexibility, scalability, and compatibility required by modern software development methodologies. They can lead to:

* **Lengthy Test Cycles**: Legacy frameworks may require manual intervention, leading to prolonged testing phases that slow down the entire CI/CD process.
* **Incompatibility with Modern Technologies**: Older frameworks might not support the latest programming languages, frameworks, or technologies, forcing teams to maintain separate testing environments or hindering the adoption of new technologies.
* **Higher Maintenance Costs**: The effort required to maintain, update, and troubleshoot legacy testing frameworks can be substantial, diverting resources away from more strategic initiatives.
